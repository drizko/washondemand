About

Have you ever needed to get your car washed but simply didn't have time? At Wash On Demand, we bring the wash to you!

Our goal is to connect providers, owners of mobile car wash and detailing services with customers who need those services.

Once you've downloaded the app and logged in or created an account, you will be redirected to the main page. As a customer, you will see options for vehicle type, wash type, and specific wash services. After selecting these, press the request a wash button to be redirected to the current wash page.

As a provider, you will see a list of nearby pending requests. Upon accepting one the, provider will be redirected to the accepted wash page, where you and the customer can view each other's contact information. Provider's can also open a map from their location to to the customer's

After arriving at the customer's location and confirming your appointment, press the start wash button. When the service is finished, press the wash complete button.
